Nepal

8 Men, 75 miles, 12K feet
54 people a day sold into slavery

Think about this

Every day more than 54 young girls and women are trafficked out of Nepal and into India to enter a life of slavery. Imagine if this was your town, your city, your state, your nation. If 54 children went missing in your community there would be an outcry.

Yet in Nepal, this is life. This is what happens every day.

Imagine with me, that we are able to reach one heart that turns away from this human trafficking. Imagine that we are able to save just one girl from this destination of slavery. This is my hope on the trip.

What are we doing?

As many of you know, I have gone to Haiti for the past 3 years, this year God is calling me somewhere different. He is calling me, with 7 other guys, to Nepal to minister to unreached people groups in April. We are partnering with an organization that has a heart for reaching the unreached people of Nepal.
